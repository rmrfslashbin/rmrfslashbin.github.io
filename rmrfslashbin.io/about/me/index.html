    Hi, I’m Robert!#

    I’m a Lifelong Technologist#

    My first computer was a Tandy TRS-80 CoCo 2 and I lived for every issue of Rainbow Magazine . I taught myself various programming languages: BASIC, assembly, C, C++, Pascal, Java, Javascript, Python, and Go. In my early teenage years, I acquired my first PC running MS-DOS . Eventually, I found Red Hat Linux 5.1 , paved over Windows , and never looked back!

    Github#

    My public Github repos contain code I’ve released into the wild. Most of my day-to-day code lives in private and corporate repos: proprietary, sensitive, or not ready for the world to see. I also have a pile of Github Gists which serve as my nerd notes.

    My Professional Life#

    I’ve spent most of my career in Systems Engineering (Windows, UNIX, VMWare ESX, and Linux). After taking and passing the Red Hat Performance Tuning exam, I decided to move into a leadership position, managing teams of cross-discipline technologists. In my current role, I lead an IT operations organization at McKesson and love the challenge. My leadership philosophy focuses on the three concepts: empowerment, mentorship, and inclusion.
